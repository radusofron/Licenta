The idea behind the project

In a world full of people who love travelling, we thought to make their life easier. The desire was to make an application for travaelling without needing anything else than this app.

What is this app capable of?

Imagine there is an app which incorporates many applications for travelling useful for travelling like Google Maps, Wikipedia, etc. Well, this app exists, and this is it.

More things about it

Find you next city to visit, plan your vacation and plan the touristic objectives very easily. The app will help you finding the best solutions for almost anything in the area of travelling.
